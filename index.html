Diagnostic Checklist: Is it time for a process review?

If you find yourself nodding to three or more of these points, your operations are leaking time and margin:

  • Work gets delayed, repeated, or stuck, but you cannot clearly see where or why.
  • Your team relies on "tribal knowledge"—if a key person is away, things grind to a halt.
  • Responsibilities are blurry; tasks fall through the cracks because no one formally owns them.
  • Important operational information is scattered across emails, loose notes, and disjointed software.
  • You feel like your current systems cannot handle taking on more volume or scaling up.
